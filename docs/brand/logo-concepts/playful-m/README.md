# Manse playful M logo concepts

Generated on 2026-07-21 with the built-in OpenAI image generation tool.

## Direction

The user-selected reference was treated as visual direction only. Every concept merges the joyful motion figure into the first lowercase `m` of the `manse` wordmark, rather than placing a separate symbol beside the name.

Shared constraints: exact lowercase word `manse` once; warm cream background; charcoal, coral, and small lime or yellow accents; flat vector-style construction; no gradients, mockups, 3D, shadows, extra text, or watermark.

## Prompt set

1. **Running kick** — The first `m` is a joyful runner: two humps become raised arms, a circular head sits in the central negative space, and one terminal becomes a springy running leg.
2. **Continuous bounce** — One continuous bouncy stroke forms the `m`; its stems become arms, its center frames the head, and its baseline flows directly into `anse`.
3. **Jumping person** — The two arches of the `m` become celebratory arms, a circular counter becomes the head, and the lower terminals become small airborne feet.
4. **Dancing ribbon** — A softer rubbery `m` uses raised arches as arms and wobbling terminals as dance steps, then connects directly into the `a`.
5. **Tracking dots** — The first `m` combines coral arm arches, a lime head dot, and two restrained foot dots while preserving the same stroke logic as `anse`.
