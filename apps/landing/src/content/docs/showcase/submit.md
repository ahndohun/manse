Publish there. Get discovered here. There is no central account, upload API, or publishing token: a normal open-source contribution is the entire submission system. Manse lists games; it does not host them.

## What happens next

1. **Manifest resolves.** CI confirms public HTTPS game and manifest URLs.
2. **Contract passes.** Schema, compatibility, identity, URLs, license, access fields, and provenance.
3. **Maintainer reviews.** A human checks semantic safety and whether disclosures match.
4. **Snapshot ships.** Approved metadata joins the Showcase and links to your creator Site.

Start from the [contribution guide in the repository](https://github.com/ahndohun/manse/blob/main/docs/CONTRIBUTING-GAMES.md), or let the `submit-to-showcase` skill in [Manse Creator](/create/install/) prepare the catalog contribution for you.
